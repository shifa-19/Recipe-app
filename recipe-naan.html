Naan

Soft, fluffy Indian flatbread baked on a hot tawa or in a tandoor, often served with curries.

Rating: ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚òÜ

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp oil or melted butter
  • Water (as required for kneading)

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add yogurt and oil, then knead into a soft dough using water as needed.
  3. Cover and let it rest for 2‚Äì3 hours.
  4. Divide dough into balls and roll into oval/round naan shapes.
  5. Heat a tawa and cook naan on one side until bubbles appear, then flip and cook until golden.
  6. Brush with butter or ghee and serve hot with curry or dal.
