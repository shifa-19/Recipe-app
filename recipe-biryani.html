Biryani

Aromatic and flavorful rice dish cooked with spices, herbs, and meat or vegetables.

Rating: ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken/mutton/vegetables
  • 2 large onions (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup curd
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 3-4 green chilies (slit)
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon)
  • 2 tsp biryani masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Fresh coriander & mint leaves
  • Saffron strands soaked in milk
  • Oil / ghee as needed
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes. Cook until 70% done, drain, and keep aside.
  2. Heat oil/ghee in a pan, fry onions until golden brown (reserve some for garnish).
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, and tomatoes. Cook until soft.
  4. Add chicken/mutton/vegetables, spices, curd, and mix well. Cook until meat is half done.
  5. Layer cooked rice over the meat/vegetable masala.
  6. Sprinkle fried onions, saffron milk, mint, and coriander leaves.
  7. Cover tightly and cook on low flame (dum) for 20‚Äì25 minutes.
  8. Serve hot with raita and salad.
