Grilled Chicken Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless chicken breasts
  • Mixed salad greens
  • Cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • 1 cucumber (sliced)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper (to taste)
  • Lemon juice or salad dressing

Instructions

  1. Season chicken breasts with olive oil, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Grill the chicken until fully cooked (about 6‚Äì7 minutes per side).
  3. Let the chicken rest, then slice it into strips.
  4. Arrange salad greens, tomatoes, and cucumber in a bowl.
  5. Top with grilled chicken slices.
  6. Drizzle with lemon juice or your favorite dressing and serve fresh.
