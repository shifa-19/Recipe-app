Chapathi

Soft and healthy Indian flatbread made from whole wheat flour, served with curries and sabzis.

Rating: ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚òÜ

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour (atta)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup water (adjust as needed)
  • 1 tsp oil or ghee (optional)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix wheat flour and salt.
  2. Add water gradually and knead into a soft, smooth dough.
  3. Cover and rest the dough for 20‚Äì30 minutes.
  4. Divide into small balls and roll into thin circles using a rolling pin.
  5. Heat a tawa (flat pan) and cook chapathis on both sides until golden spots appear.
  6. Optionally apply ghee/butter and serve hot with curry or sabzi.
