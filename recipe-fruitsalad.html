Fruit Salad

A refreshing mix of seasonal fruits, perfect for any time of the day.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup watermelon cubes
  • 1 cup apple slices
  • 1 cup banana slices
  • ¬Ω cup grapes
  • 1 orange (peeled and segmented)
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • ¬Ω tsp lemon juice
  • A pinch of chaat masala (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash, peel, and cut all fruits into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Place them in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Drizzle honey and lemon juice over the fruits.
  4. Add a pinch of chaat masala for extra flavor (optional).
  5. Toss gently and serve fresh & chilled.
