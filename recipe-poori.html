Poori

Fluffy deep-fried Indian bread, best served with curry.

Rating: ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 tbsp semolina (optional)
  • ¬Ω tsp salt
  • Water (as required for dough)
  • Oil for deep frying

Steps

  1. Mix flour, semolina, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Knead into a firm dough using water.
  3. Divide into small balls and roll into circles.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry until golden and puffed.
  5. Serve hot with potato curry or chole.
